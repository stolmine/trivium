## üß† Scientific Spaced Repetition

### FSRS-5 Algorithm
State-of-the-art scheduling algorithm that adapts to your learning patterns. More accurate than traditional SM-2, optimizing review timing for maximum retention.

### Flexible Review Sessions
Study exactly what you want:
- Filter by folder, text, or global scope
- Set daily limits for new cards and reviews
- Review in random or creation order
- Bury difficult cards for later

### Comprehensive Grading
Four-button grading system (Again, Hard, Good, Easy) with keyboard shortcuts:
- **Again (1)**: Review again soon
- **Hard (2)**: Slightly increase interval
- **Good (3)**: Normal progression
- **Easy (4)**: Much longer interval

### Review Controls
- **Undo Last Review**: Made a wrong choice? Press Ctrl+Z
- **Bury Cards**: Shift+B to hide cards until next session
- **Progress Tracking**: See cards remaining and session progress
- **1000+ Card Capacity**: Handle massive review sessions without slowdown
